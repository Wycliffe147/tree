At her home a girl lived
Hopless and lost she alwalys grieved
For truth be told
Like evryone else
She had a soul
Only that hers
was not as it was
When God gave it whole

She used to ponder the earth
Living her life to the fullest
For what use is life
If you don't live it?
There is need to enjoy
all its pleasure
Before we drop down kulichete
Life is meant to be lived
This she trully believed
With a hie from sin to sinness
.....A morning to prayer procastination
.....An afternoon to eating weakness
.....And night of bad imagination

Was there no hope
For this lost soul?
A whole troupe
thought so
But the way maker
Made a way
A way out of this soul's slavery

The way maker
Made a route on the river Jordan
For this girl to cross.
The route led her to a Jericho
A Jericho called Life
A Jericho for her to conqure
And conqure it she did
With a hie from Faith to Faith
.....A morning to praying devotion
.....An afternoon to fasting bread
.....And night of devine communication

Now the girl went back home
But her past was dead
For a new dawn rose through
And her name was you.

The End

~Made by Wycliffe Mwanagnda
